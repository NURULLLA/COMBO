# How to Publish Your App (Free)

Since you cannot build an iOS app on Windows without a Mac, this **Web App** is the perfect solution. It works exactly like an app when added to your home screen.

## Step 1: Create a GitHub Repository
1.  Log in to your [GitHub account](https://github.com).
2.  Click the **+** icon in the top-right and select **New repository**.
3.  Name it `trim757` (or whatever you like).
4.  Make sure it is **Public**.
5.  Click **Create repository**.

## Step 2: Upload Files
1.  In your new repository, look for the link that says **uploading an existing file**.
2.  Open the folder `web_app` on your computer.
3.  **Drag and Drop** the following files into the browser window:
    *   `index.html`
    *   `script.js`
    *   `style.css`
    *   `icon.png`
4.  Wait for them to finish uploading.
5.  Click **Commit changes** (green button at the bottom).

## Step 3: Turn on the Website
1.  In your repository, click on the **Settings** tab (gear icon).
2.  On the left sidebar, click **Pages** (under the "Code and automation" section).
3.  Under **Build and deployment** > **Branch**:
    *   Select `main` (or `master`) from the dropdown.
    *   Ensure the folder is set to `/(root)`.
    *   Click **Save**.
4.  Wait about 1-2 minutes. Refresh the page.
5.  You will see a bar at the top saying: **"Your site is live at..."**

## Step 4: Install on Phone (iPhone/Android)
1.  Send that link to your phone (email, WhatsApp, etc.).
2.  **iPhone (iOS)**:
    *   Open the link in **Safari**.
    *   Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow up).
    *   Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
    *   Tap **Add**.
3.  **Android**:
    *   Open the link in **Chrome**.
    *   Tap the three dots menu (top right).
    *   Tap **Add to Home screen** (or "Install App").

**Result**: You now have the Trim Calculator icon on your home screen. It opens full-screen and works offline!
